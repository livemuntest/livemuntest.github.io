Who are we?

Today’s youth are the force, hope and leaders of tomorrow. Our mission is to well-equip these youth by making MUN available for all! LiveMUN is a free online platform dedicated to connecting students from all around the world to discuss topics ranging from politics to economics to popculture. 

Since LiveMUN was released in March 2020, hundreds of delegates have joined from all around the world making it the first and biggest online MUN based in Lebanon. The LiveMUN staff welcomes you and can’t wait for you to become part of the LiveMUN family!

Training Sessions

In order to make everyone feel comfortable with the conference, the LiveMUN staff hosts Training Sessions a couple of weeks before each batch of conferences. These sessions are great for newcomers who want to learn more about how MUN works or for experienced delegates who want to brush over the Rules of Procedure! 

24/7 Support on Instagram DMs

The LiveMUN Staff is always here to help! Just direct any questions or inquiries you might have towards our Instagram Account's Direct Messaging (DMs). We also host a lot of challenges there so don’t forget to check out our page @live.mun.
